Over 65 Languages One Easy to Use Platform. No Monthly Fees

EQHO has been delivering fast, accurate translations to global brands for 20 years. We harness the power of our proprietary translation technology, which enables our team of 3,500 linguists to deliver your project quickly and cost effectively.

Features

  • Receive Instant Quotations

    Copy paste your text or upload text-based files, and our proprietary counter will calculate your project

  • Intuitive User Interface

    Review everything with ease within an easy to use mobile friendly dashboard

  • Track your Project Progress

    Track your project from translation to review, revision and completion

  • Build and Maintain Lists of your Favorite Translators

    Maintain translator IDs to enable you to reuse them on your future projects

  • Receive Real Time Alerts when it’s Review Time

    Receive real-time alerts when the translations and amendments are completed

  • Maintain and Manage your Project Assets

    Maintain your terminology glossaries, brand guides and other assets all in one place

Our Promise

Smart technology means you translate once

Never pay for the same sentence to be translated twice. Our smart technology recognizes repeated text and only serves new sentences to the translator so you save time and costs

A 4-stage human-centric process

Personal Translation Assistant assigns the right linguist to your job.A native-speaking subject-specialist translator is placed according to subject type. A senior editor fine-tunes the translation, paying close attention to accuracy and style. A wiz-kid layout artist formats your file.
